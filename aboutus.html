About Us

Polbras Environment is a project focused on sustainability and eco- friendly solutions. The highlight is on the importance of recycling, green energy and reforestation. The goal is to raise awareness and inspire action for a greener future. Our app is a place that brings together all the people who like and want to do more for the environment. You can consume content about environmental tips and sustainability and share your knowledge with other people. You will have tips on how to care for a specific plant, content about recycling and organizing get-togethers. Our app aims to encourage a sustainable lifestyle.
